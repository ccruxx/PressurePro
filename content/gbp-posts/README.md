## Usage Tips

1. **Frequency:** Post 2-3 times per month on Google Business Profile
2. **Timing:** Best engagement on weekday mornings (8-10 AM) and Saturday afternoons
3. **Images:** Always include before/after photos when available
4. **Local Focus:** Rotate between different cities to maintain relevance across service areas
5. **Seasonal:** Adjust messaging for spring (house washing), summer (concrete), fall (roof cleaning)
6. **Call Tracking:** Monitor which posts drive the most phone calls and double down on those topics
